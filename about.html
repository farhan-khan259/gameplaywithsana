About Chess Master

Pakistan’s First Platform for Flying Chess Tournaments

Our Story

Chess Arena is Pakistan’s first-ever dedicated platform for Flying Chess competitions — built to discover, celebrate, and unite strategic thinkers from every corner of the country. Our mission is to create a free, welcoming, and community-driven space where players of all ages can compete, showcase their skills, and grow through fun, healthy competition.

Our Mission

Whether you’re a lifelong board game lover or just enjoy a good mental challenge, Chess Master is for you. No registration fees, no complicated rules — just exciting, fair, and action-packed tournaments open to everyone.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of eco-friendly, inclusive, and engaging board game events in Pakistan, inspiring communities to embrace fair play, develop strategic thinking, and connect with others through shared passion for games.

What is Flying Chess?

Flying Chess (also known as Aeroplane Chess) is a classic multiplayer board game that combines logic and strategy. Played by 2 to 4 players using dice and tokens, the goal is to race your pieces from the base to the finish line while blocking or overtaking opponents. We’ve elevated this traditional game into a competitive, skill-based tournament format.

Why We Started Chess Master

Pakistan is full of talented individuals who enjoy classic games but lack structured platforms to compete. Chess Master was created to change that — providing a space where everyone can participate without financial barriers, and where strategy and skill are celebrated.
